This is a demo for Day 10 of a twelve-part chess-themed tutorial on writing an SPA in Bucklescript-TEA.

Make moves by drag and drop or use the buttons. Resize the window and watch the board grow and shrink, and the move list be rearranged. Click the "Tournament" tab to load a list of games from Lichess and open a game. The game will be parsed and you can replay it. Try saving the games into the browser's local storage. They will be restored when you close the window and open it again. Watch the browser's URL bar.
